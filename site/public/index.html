Built for real investigations

Ingest almost anything

CSV, TSV/TXT, Excel, JSON, GeoJSON, KML/KMZ, GPX — even a ZIP of all of them. Columns are auto-detected with confidence scores, and you can map them by hand when a return is unusual.

Evidence integrity, always

Every source file is SHA-256 hashed and preserved byte-for-byte. Rows are flagged, never deleted. A tamper-evident audit hash chain records every import, processing run, and export.

Clean & validate automatically

Coordinates parsed, timestamps normalized to UTC and converted to your display time zone, duplicates and reversed lat/lon flagged, low-accuracy and time-zone-uncertain points called out.

Court-ready outputs

Interactive map with street/satellite/hybrid views and date-time filtering, cleaned CSV, Google Earth KML/KMZ, GeoJSON, and a PDF processing report with hashes, counts, and a time-zone statement.

Investigator AI assistant Pro

Ask plain-English questions: "where did the device dwell longest?", "any gaps or impossible jumps?" Answers locally by default; optionally uses a hosted model with only aggregate context — never the full record set.

Case workspace & training mode

Organize uploads and exports into local cases with a verifiable audit log. Practice the whole workflow on bundled fake data — every training output is clearly watermarked.

Frequently asked questions

Does my evidence data leave my computer?

No. Processing, mapping, hashing, and exports all happen locally. The only optional network feature is the Pro AI assistant's hosted model, which receives an aggregate summary (counts, time range, a small sample) — never your full record set — and can be disabled entirely.

Is it defensible in court?

GeoBrief is built around evidence integrity: SHA-256 hashes of originals, byte-for-byte preservation, flag-don't-delete cleaning, and a tamper-evident audit hash chain you can verify at any time. The PDF report documents every hash, count, warning, and time-zone decision. As with any tool, admissibility decisions rest with your prosecutor and court.

What happens when my subscription lapses?

Your license key stops unlocking paid features shortly after the billing period ends. Your data is yours — cases, originals, and exports stay on your machine and remain readable.

Can my whole unit use it?

Each subscription licenses one investigator's installation. For agency-wide deployment or self-hosted servers, contact us.
