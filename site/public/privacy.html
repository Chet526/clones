Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Your evidence data stays local

GeoBrief LE runs on your own computer or server. Files you process, cases you create, maps, exports, and audit logs are stored locally and are never transmitted to us. We have no access to your evidence data.

The optional AI assistant

By default the assistant answers entirely on your machine. If you configure a hosted model (an OpenRouter API key), only an aggregate analysis context is sent — record counts, a time range, data-quality flags, a movement summary, and a small sample of points — never your full record set. You control this with environment variables and can disable it at any time.

What we collect on this website

  • Purchases: checkout and payment are handled by Stripe. We receive your email address and subscription status so we can issue your license key. We never see your card details.
  • License keys: generated from your plan, email, and billing period; delivered to you and not stored by us.

We do not run third-party ad trackers on this site.

Data retention & your rights

Billing records are retained by Stripe per their policies and as required for tax and accounting. To cancel your subscription, request deletion of your billing contact information, or ask questions, contact us via the email on your receipt.
